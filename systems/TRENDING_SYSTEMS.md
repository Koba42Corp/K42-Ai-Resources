## üí° Key Insights

1. **Open Source is Winning**: Developers prefer open-source alternatives (Supabase over Firebase, PostgreSQL over proprietary)
2. **Type Safety Matters**: TypeScript, tRPC, and type-safe APIs are becoming standard
3. **AI Integration is Essential**: Most modern products need AI capabilities
4. **Developer Experience is Critical**: Tools with great DX (Vercel, Resend, Clerk) are winning
5. **Full-Stack Frameworks**: Next.js, Remix, and similar are replacing separate frontend/backend
6. **Managed Services**: Developers want managed services but with control (Supabase, Railway)
